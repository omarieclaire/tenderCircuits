Knot Project Space Podcast 4

In the fourth and final episode of ‘in Space Grey’, we talk with Tina Pearson about Deep Listening®, Tina's approaches to scoring music, and her thoughts on performing with fielding recordings; and Tosca Terán discusses mycelium networks and her process of collaborating with living fungi to create soundscapes and generative visuals.

in Space Grey is a durational mostly a-synchronous online exhibition meditating on themes of connection, environmental extraction and accelerated capitalism. Presenting works by Ashley Bowa & Lesley Marshall, WhiteFeather Hunter, Maize Longboat, Tina Pearson, Manuel Piña-Baldoquín, Emilio Portal and Tosca Teran.
