Seth Thomson

Seth Thomson is an artist and creative technologist based in Ottawa, Ontario. Coming from a technical background in a Bachelors of Information Technology program at Carleton University, he is interested in the impact of technology on art. Recent work has included directing Be/Loved at the Fresh Meat theatre festival. Further work includes digital development on Cabine, an interactive production at Zones Théâtrales set inside a phone booth. He is also a member of Bizarre Love Triangle, a multidisciplinary arts collective of emerging artists working at the intersection of art and technology.
