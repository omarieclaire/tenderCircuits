Vasuki Shanmuganathan

Vasuki Shanmuganathan is an artist, researcher, and educator. Her art and curatorial practice engages with archives of the mundane and symbiotic forms of connections such as communal care using material and immaterial means to trace the borders of the digital sphere. She holds a PhD from the University of Toronto with a dissertation examining race, aesthetics, and colonialism. In 2016, she started the Tamil Archive Project (TAP) artist collective, which combines art, knowledge translation, and archival practices into accessible events that center care. She has curated and exhibited at The Public Gallery, Lakeshore Arts, Scarborough Arts, and Make Room Inc in 2021.
