Helen Lam

Helen Lam is an Ottawa-based narrative designer with experience in the video game and virtual reality mediums. As a new artist, she brings perspectives from a variety of mediums to each project, keen to experiment with the boundaries of each technology. Her throughline is always finding the value in the narrative structures across mediums and experiences. She is fascinated with extended reality technology (virtual reality, augmented reality etc.) and their impact on modern society. Her previous project, a collaborative video game made in 48 hours, was featured in the 2021 Ars Electronica Festival.
